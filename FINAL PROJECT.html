Ownership Background and History

Who we are, September 25, 2018; 6:08pm

The JJ Sovereign Corporation was established in June 13, 2016 by Eejay Ta-a and his colleague, John Lloyd Alvarez. JJ Sovereign Co. is the leading and most popular fashion house in the Philippines.

From our perspective, JJ Sovereign Co. is the Filipino answer to global fashion. It incorporates the love and passion for fashion, quality, and design, together with the Filipino character whose source is in our nature as people and as a fashion house, energetic, colorful, urban, free, happy, and sexy, with a pinch of chutzpah.

As the leading and most popular fashion house in the Philippines, JJ Sovereign Co. is proud to lead a productive and ongoing dialogue between its local design studio and the people who live here in the Philippines. This is a fruitful conversation created by people who live here, for people who live here. This is our home. As a youthful company for fashion and lifestyle, we are committed to our values and provide our customers a unique and exciting experience in all areas of personal appearance and lifestyle. We see in this a commitment of respect and appreciation for our customers.

Our collections harmonize fashionable design, sophistication, and excitement, with quality, richness, and care, a combination that gives our customers complete confidence in their choices, and fully addresses their fashion needs.

JJ Sovereign Co. is committed to not only meeting but also exceeding all customer requirements, thus ensuring a 100% quality target that is carried throughout each step of the manufacturing process. The end result leads to the complete satisfaction of every customer and enhanced growth and success of the company.

We consider it an extremely important goal to continue to lead fashion in the Philippines through the next decade, to closely accompany our customers in all areas of appearance and lifestyle, through the design and sale of fashion and lifestyle products of the first rank, and at fair and accessible prices.
